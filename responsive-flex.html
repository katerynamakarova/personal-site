RECOMENDATIONS

Engineer Tim embarks on journey to atone for the fatal act of his life. During his trip, he meets seven strangers, including the terminally ill Emily, who calls herself a girl with black wings.
Based on the true story of Saroo Brierley who, at the age of 5, was separated from his family in India as he fell asleep on a train and woke up 1600 kilometers away from home.
